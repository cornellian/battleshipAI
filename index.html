Instructions:

You have five ships: a Carrier (C), of length 5, a Battleship (B), of length 4, a Destroyer (D), of length 3, a Submarine (S), of length 3, and a Gunboat (G), of length 2. Place all five ships within the grid denoted by the uppercase first letter of each ship name. No ships may overlap. Ships are allowed to be touching.

The "Random Board" feature isn't implemented yet- right now, it'll start the game with a specific board setup I used for testing.
